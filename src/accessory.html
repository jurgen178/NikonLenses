This page lists accessories for your Nikkor lenses. Never apply excessive force when fitting accessories. Incorrect usage could affect your image or damage your lens!

  • Type - Lens version or model
  • Lens - Lens focal length and designation
  • Filter - Filter attachment size / Internal filter (mm)
    r - Filter rotates when focusing
    z - Filter rotates when zooming
    rear - Rear bayonet filter
    gel - Gelatine filter holder
  • Hood - Recommended hood, more details here.
    HN - Metal screw in (Nejikomi) hood
    HR - Rubber screw in hood
    HS - Snap-on hood
    HK - Slip-on (Kabuse) hood
    HE - Hood Extension
    HB - Plastic Bayonet hood
  • Alt Hood - If the recommended hood is not available or a different style is preferred, some alternatives are listed. Hoods for lenses with similar or shorter focal lengths can generally be interchanged.
    * May cause slight vignetting if fitted over a filter or at wider zoom settings
    ^ Useable on DX format cameras
  • Case - Lens case recommended for the lens at the time of production.
    Many different cases have been produced, catalogues often have errors or are inconsistent - use whatever case fits!
    CL - Black leatherette cylindrical case with soft lining
    CT - Trunk Case supplied with larger telephotos
    CP - Plastic bubble case (discontinued)
    No-xx, CL-Sxx and CL-Mxx are soft pouch cases
    * Useable with matching extension tube attached
    ^ Useable when tripod mount is removed
  • Teleconverters. Warning: fitting a lens with an incompatible TC could damage your lens or TC
    201 - TC-201(AiS) - TC-200(Ai) - TC-1(pre-Ai)
    301 - TC-301(AiS) - TC-300(Ai) - TC-2(pre-Ai)
    14B - TC-14B(AiS) - TC-14(Ai)
    o - Teleconverter is usable
    v - Vignetting or other image deterioration may occur at close focus or wide apertures
    u - Uneven exposure may result at high shutter speed with small aperture
    r - Usable if rear filter is removed
    a - Autofocus is possible
  • Other - Other accessories included or recommended
